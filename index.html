How It Works

From Assessment Data to
Compliant IEP in 4 Steps

1

Upload & Import

Upload assessment data (iReady, NWEA, state tests) or import from connected systems. Our parsers extract key metrics automatically.

2

Collect Input

Role-specific questionnaires guide parents, students, and teachers through structured input — available in multiple languages.

3

AI Generates IEP

Our trained AI models synthesize all data into compliant PLAAFP statements, measurable annual goals, and appropriate accommodations.

4

Review & Finalize

The IEP team reviews the generated document, makes edits, and approves. The final IEP is stored securely in the digital file cabinet.
